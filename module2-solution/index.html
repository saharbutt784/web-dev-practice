Chicken Biryani

An iconic South Asian delicacy, Biryani is a royal blend of long-grain basmati rice and tender pieces of marinated meat or vegetables, layered with caramelized onions, saffron, fresh herbs, and a unique mix of aromatic spices. Slow-cooked in a sealed pot (dum-style) to lock in flavor and fragrance, each bite offers a perfect harmony of richness and depth. Served with cooling raita or tangy salan, this dish is a complete and satisfying culinary experience.
